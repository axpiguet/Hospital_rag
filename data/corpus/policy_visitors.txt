TITLE: Visitor Policy
DEPARTMENT: Patient Services
LAST REVIEWED: February 2026

GENERAL WARDS
Visiting hours are 10:00 to 20:00 daily. A maximum of two visitors per patient is permitted at the bedside at any time. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times and are not permitted in shared rooms during active treatment procedures.

INTENSIVE CARE UNIT (ICU)
Visiting hours are restricted to 11:00-13:00 and 17:00-19:00. Only one visitor is permitted at the bedside at a time, and visits are limited to 20 minutes to allow for clinical care. Visitors must perform hand hygiene and may be asked to wear personal protective equipment depending on the patient's isolation status.

MATERNITY WARD
Partners or one designated support person may stay overnight. Other visitors are limited to 12:00-19:00. Siblings of the newborn are welcome but must be supervised at all times.

INFECTION CONTROL EXCEPTIONS
During declared outbreak periods (e.g. seasonal influenza surges), visitor numbers may be further restricted by the Infection Prevention and Control team, and updates will be posted at ward entrances.

PARKING AND ACCESS
Visitors should use the main entrance on Rue de l'Hopital. Validated parking vouchers are available at the reception desk for stays under 2 hours.
